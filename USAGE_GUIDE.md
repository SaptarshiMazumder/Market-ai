# Usage Guide - Market AI Image Generator

## How to Generate Professional Product Images

### Method 1: Text-to-Image (No Reference)

Perfect for creating completely new product visualizations from scratch.

**Steps:**
1. Enter a detailed product description
2. Choose a style preset
3. Click "Generate Image"

**Example:**
- Description: "luxury wristwatch with silver band and blue dial, leather texture"
- Style: Luxury
- Result: Brand new product image generated from scratch

### Method 2: Image-to-Image (With Reference) ‚≠ê Recommended

Use this when you have an existing product photo and want to enhance it or create variations.

**Steps:**
1. Upload your product photo as reference
2. Enter a description (can be same as original or enhanced)
3. Choose a style preset
4. Click "Generate Image"

**How it works:**
- The AI uses your reference image as a base
- Maintains composition, lighting, and overall structure
- Applies your style preset and description
- Creates a professional, enhanced version

**Example Use Cases:**

#### Use Case 1: Enhance Product Quality
- **Reference:** Basic product photo with cluttered background
- **Description:** "luxury smartwatch with black screen and leather strap, professional studio lighting"
- **Style:** Professional
- **Result:** Clean, professional marketing image with perfect lighting

#### Use Case 2: Style Transformation
- **Reference:** Plain product photo
- **Description:** "premium perfume bottle with golden accents"
- **Style:** Luxury
- **Result:** Elevated, high-end product image

#### Use Case 3: Background Cleanup
- **Reference:** Product photo with messy background
- **Description:** "wireless earbuds in charging case, clean white background"
- **Style:** Professional
- **Result:** Product isolated on clean white background

#### Use Case 4: Multiple Variations
- **Reference:** Same product photo
- **Description:** Keep same description
- **Different Styles:** Generate with Professional, Luxury, Modern, and Vibrant
- **Result:** 4 different marketing images for different campaigns

## Style Guide

### Professional
- **Best for:** Corporate, B2B, e-commerce
- **Look:** Clean, minimal, white background, corporate feel
- **Use when:** You need standard product catalog images

### Luxury
- **Best for:** Premium products, high-end brands
- **Look:** Elegant, sophisticated, premium materials visible
- **Use when:** Selling luxury items, premium positioning

### Modern
- **Best for:** Tech products, contemporary brands
- **Look:** Sleek, trendy, contemporary aesthetics
- **Use when:** Targeting younger, style-conscious audience

### Vibrant
- **Best for:** Lifestyle products, social media
- **Look:** Colorful, eye-catching, energetic
- **Use when:** Creating attention-grabbing ads

## Troubleshooting

**Generated image doesn't match:**
- Be more specific in description
- Use a reference image for better guidance
- Try different style presets

**Image quality not good enough:**
- Ensure reference image is high quality
- Add "professional studio lighting, 8k uhd, high quality" to description
- Try the Luxury or Professional styles

**Product looks wrong:**
- Your description might be too different from reference
- Try being more specific about what to keep/change
- Generate multiple variations
